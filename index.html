Commands and Features

play Lets you play any music you want
filter Set effects for the audio
radio Play a radiostation from 30+ stations
seek +/- 10 Lets you seek audio +/- 10s by clicking reactions
favorite(dev) Make a private playlist of your favorite music
leave Tells the bot to leave your channel
search Lets you search songs
pause Lets you pause the bot
resume Lets you resume the bot
skip Lets you skip the current track
stop Lets you stop the bot
now playing Shows you all information about the current track
lyrics Shows you lyrics from the current track
queue Shows you each song inside the queue
volume Lets you change the volume
shuffle Lets you activate shuffling
loop Lets you activate the loop
move Lets you move a song from one position to another
remove Lets you remove a specific song
prefix Lets you change the prefix for that server
reset(dev) Lets you reset the current progress
help Shows you all commands and their aliases
uptime Shows you uptime of the bot
invite Shows you an invite for Harmony
support Shows you an invite to the support server
vote(dev) Shows you some information about upvoting
donate(dev) Shows you some information about donating
